20 Low Cost House-Decorating Ideas

Ideas that call for a little imagination, and little money, add instant style to any decor.

  1. Canvas the Area

    2. Hang canvases painted with interior latex paint to brighten blank walls. Think big: The canvases should fill the wall.

  2. Create A Side Table

    3. Stack modular storage units (from $22, amazon.com) to make a stepped side table with surfaces for displays and lighting. Create miniature still lifes in the cubbies, or stack reading material that you?re going to get to...someday.

  3. Hang Plates
